About

I am a full-time undergraduate student studying cognitive science at UC Berkeley. My career goal is to pursue software development professionally. I am interested in AI/ML implementations of softwares that can solve everyday problems, such as speech-to-text apps to support those with disabilities and better technological systems for healthcare services.

In addition to computer science, I am enthusiastic about music. As a kid, I fell in love with classical piano and studied through private lessons for over ten years. Some highlights from that time include performing Schumann's Piano Concerto in A Minor with Music Fest Perugia's orchestra in Perugia, Italy (2022); assisting the Folsom Lake College Youth Chamber Orchestra (FLCYCO) as their pianist (2021-2023); and playing Beethoven's Cello Sonata No. 3 at my senior year recital. Now, I played keyboard, guitar, and saxophone in various school groups. Currently, I am crazy about learning to play jazz guitar.
